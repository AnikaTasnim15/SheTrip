Share Your Location

Sharing your live location with a trusted contact can increase your safety when meeting someone in person.

How to share safely

  • Use a trusted app (Google Maps, Apple Find My, WhatsApp live location) to share with one or two trusted contacts.
  • Only share for the period you expect to be in transit or at the meeting location.
  • Do not share your location publicly; only share with a verified friend or family member.

If you can't share live location, send your planned route and expected arrival time to your contact.
