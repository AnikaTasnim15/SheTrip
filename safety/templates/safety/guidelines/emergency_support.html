Emergency Support

In case of emergency, follow these steps:

  1. Move to a safe place if possible (public area, store, or well-lit street).
  2. Call local emergency services immediately.
  3. Contact your emergency contacts and share your location.
  4. If the situation involves a SheTrip user, report the profile and the incident through the Safety Reports page.

Emergency numbers:

  • Bangladesh: 999
  • Always check local emergency numbers when traveling abroad.
