Verify Before Meeting

Before you meet someone from the community, follow these checks:

  1. Check their profile: verified badges, reviews, and completed trips.
  2. Confirm identity via social links or phone verification when possible.
  3. Arrange to meet in a public place and share the meet time with friends.
  4. Set a short check-in time with the person and make sure your emergency contacts know the plan.

If anything feels off, cancel the meet and report the profile.
